About MediCare

MediCare is a healthcare management platform designed to connect patients and doctors through a secure and user-friendly system.

Our Mission

To simplify healthcare management by providing secure access to appointments, medical records, and doctor-patient communication.

What We Offer

  • Doctor Registration & Approval
  • Patient Management
  • Appointment Booking
  • Medical Report Upload & Viewing
  • Encrypted Medical Records
  • Role-Based Access Control

Technology Stack

Built using Angular, Spring Boot, MySQL, JWT Authentication, and secure report management.
